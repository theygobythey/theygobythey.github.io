Jaq Nguyen Victor is the whimsy-drenched, second-generation child of a Vietnamese love warrior mother to whom they credit any and all magical gifts. They go by they. This genderqueer unicorn is pursuing graduate studies in Drama Therapy at the California Institute of Integral Studies (CIIS) and holds a Bachelor's degree in Philosophy from the University of Colorado (CU Boulder). Last year, Jaq debuted a full-length show, Gender Assimilation: A Rebuttal, at the United States of Asian America Festival (USAAF) and is currently working on two others, Decolonization is Like Hard and Princess Genderqueer & the Racist Pea. This year, they will be debuting a short film, Swanicorn: A Genderqueer Fairytale, at the Queer Women of Color Media Arts Project Film Festival (QWOCMAP) and are currently completing another, Drugs for Unicorns.
